About GST in India

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a unified indirect tax in India applied on the supply of goods and services. Our GST Calculator India helps you calculate GST-inclusive and exclusive prices in seconds. The tool supports all GST rates – 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%.

Whether you're a business owner, freelancer, or consumer, this calculator provides an easy way to understand the tax implications on your transactions. Simply enter the amount and select the GST rate to get instant results.
